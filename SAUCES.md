## Sauce 1: Garlic Herb Butter

**Code:** `garlic-herb-butter`
**Style:** Rich, savoury, classic
**Best with:** Steaks (all cuts), chicken breast, roasted vegetables, baby potatoes
**Cooking methods:** Pan, oven, BBQ

### Ingredients (per 30g serve)
- Butter, unsalted — 20g
- Garlic, fresh minced — 4g
- Flat-leaf parsley, fresh chopped — 3g
- Thyme, fresh — 1g
- Chives, fresh chopped — 1g
- Sea salt — 0.5g
- Black pepper, cracked — 0.5g

### Lactose-Free Variant
- Replace butter with lactose-free butter (same quantity)

### Allergens
- **Contains:** Dairy (milk)
- **LF variant:** Lactose-free dairy

### Dietary Flags
- GF: Yes
- DF: No (contains butter) — no dairy-free variant for this sauce
- LF: Yes (with lactose-free butter swap)

### Nutrition Per 30g Serve
| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat | Fiber | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 152 | 0.3g | 0.8g | 16.4g | 0.1g | 125mg |

### Meals Using This Sauce
- Beef Fillet with Garlic Herb Butter and Asparagus
- Pan-Seared Sirloin with Herb Butter and Baby Potatoes
- Chicken Breast with Garlic Butter and Roasted Broccoli
- Rump Steak with Herb Butter and Sweet Potato Wedges
- T-Bone with Garlic Butter and Mushrooms

### INTERNAL — Production Recipe (per 1kg batch)
- Unsalted butter: 667g (soften to room temp)
- Fresh garlic, minced: 133g
- Flat-leaf parsley, finely chopped: 100g
- Fresh thyme, leaves stripped and chopped: 33g
- Fresh chives, finely chopped: 33g
- Sea salt: 17g
- Cracked black pepper: 17g
- Method: Combine all into softened butter. Mix until evenly distributed. Roll into logs using cling wrap. Refrigerate until firm. Slice into 30g portions. Vacuum seal individual portions.
- Shelf life: 7 days refrigerated, 3 months frozen.
- Yield: ~33 portions per kg batch.

- Tamari: 320g
- Rice wine vinegar: 160g
- Sesame oil: 80g
- Fresh ginger, minced: 80g
- Fresh garlic, minced: 60g
- Water: 160g
- Arrowroot powder: 60g
- Toasted sesame seeds: 40g
- Spring onion, finely sliced: 40g

- Shelf life: 10 days refrigerated.
- Yield: ~40 portions per kg batch.

- Tomato passata: 320g
- Onion, finely diced: 160g
- Fresh garlic, minced: 80g
- Fresh ginger, minced: 80g

## Production Notes

- All sauces and marinades are produced in a halal-certified kitchen
- Ingredient sourcing must maintain halal certification chain
- No alcohol-based ingredients (wine, mirin etc.) — substitutes used throughout (vinegar, water)
- No sugar or artificial sweeteners in any sauce
- Natural sweetness from ingredients only (pear puree in Korean, tomato in BBQ)
- All pouches labelled with: sauce name, ingredients list, allergens, nutrition per serve, halal certification mark, use-by date
- Internal recipes are NEVER shared with customers or displayed on website/app

# SIDE SAUCES (Optional Add-Ons)

> These are NOT included with meals by default. Customers add them as an optional side sauce to any order. Comes in a separate pouch. Customer heats in a saucepan for 2-3 min. Cooking instructions on pouch and in app.

---

## Side Sauce 1: Creamy Mushroom Sauce

**Code:** `side-mushroom`
**Style:** Rich, creamy, earthy
**Best with:** Steaks (all cuts), chicken breast, lamb chops
**Heating:** Saucepan, 2-3 min on medium. Stir until warmed through.

### Ingredients (per 60g serve)
- Button mushrooms, finely sliced — 20g
- Thickened cream (or lactose-free cream) — 18g
- Olive oil — 3g
- Garlic, minced — 2g
- Fresh thyme — 1g
- Onion, finely diced — 5g
- Dijon mustard — 2g
- Chicken stock (halal certified) — 5g
- Sea salt — 0.5g
- Black pepper — 0.5g
- Cornflour — 1g
- Water — 2g

### Lactose-Free Variant
- Replace thickened cream with lactose-free cream (same quantity)

### Allergens
- **Contains:** Dairy (milk), Mustard

### Dietary Flags
- GF: Yes | DF: No | LF: Yes (LF cream swap)

### Nutrition Per 60g Serve
| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat | Fiber | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 85 | 1.8g | 3.2g | 7.2g | 0.4g | 180mg |

### INTERNAL — Production Recipe (per 2kg batch)
- Button mushrooms, finely sliced: 667g
- Thickened cream: 600g
- Olive oil: 100g
- Fresh garlic, minced: 67g
- Fresh thyme, stripped: 33g
- Onion, finely diced: 167g
- Dijon mustard: 67g
- Halal chicken stock: 167g
- Sea salt: 17g
- Black pepper: 17g
- Cornflour: 33g
- Water: 67g
- Method: Heat oil. Saute onion 3 min. Add garlic and mushrooms, cook 5 min until browned. Add thyme. Mix cornflour with water. Add cream, stock, mustard. Stir in cornflour slurry. Simmer 5 min. Season. Cool. Portion into 60g pouches.
- Shelf life: 5 days refrigerated, 2 months frozen.
- Yield: ~33 portions per 2kg batch.

## Side Sauce 2: Pepper Sauce

**Code:** `side-pepper`
**Style:** Bold, peppery, slightly creamy
**Best with:** Steaks (rump, sirloin, fillet), lamb leg steak
**Heating:** Saucepan, 2-3 min on medium.

### Ingredients (per 50g serve)
- Thickened cream (or lactose-free cream) — 15g
- Cracked black peppercorns — 3g
- Green peppercorns (brined, drained) — 3g
- Olive oil — 2g
- Garlic, minced — 1.5g
- Onion, finely diced — 5g
- Halal beef stock — 10g
- Dijon mustard — 1.5g
- Non-alcoholic brandy essence — 1g
- Sea salt — 0.5g
- Cornflour — 1g
- Water — 6g

### Allergens
- **Contains:** Dairy (milk), Mustard

### Dietary Flags
- GF: Yes | DF: No | LF: Yes (LF cream swap)

### Nutrition Per 50g Serve
| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat | Fiber | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 72 | 1.2g | 2.8g | 6.0g | 0.5g | 195mg |

### INTERNAL — Production Recipe (per 1.5kg batch)
- Thickened cream: 450g
- Cracked black peppercorns: 90g
- Green peppercorns (brined, drained): 90g
- Olive oil: 60g
- Fresh garlic, minced: 45g
- Onion, finely diced: 150g
- Halal beef stock: 300g
- Dijon mustard: 45g
- Non-alcoholic brandy essence: 30g
- Sea salt: 15g
- Cornflour: 30g
- Water: 180g
- Method: Heat oil, saute onion 3 min. Add garlic 1 min. Add peppercorns, toast 30 sec. Add stock and brandy essence, simmer 3 min. Cornflour slurry into sauce. Add cream and mustard. Simmer 5 min. Season. Cool. Portion 50g pouches.
- Shelf life: 5 days refrigerated, 2 months frozen.
- Yield: ~30 portions per 1.5kg batch.

## Side Sauce 3: Gravy

**Code:** `side-gravy`
**Style:** Classic, rich, savoury
**Best with:** Any roast, mash, potatoes, chicken, lamb
**Heating:** Saucepan, 2-3 min. Splash of water if too thick.

### Ingredients (per 50g serve)
- Halal beef stock — 20g
- Halal chicken stock — 10g
- Onion, finely diced — 5g
- Garlic, minced — 1g
- Olive oil — 2g
- Cornflour — 2g
- Fresh thyme — 0.5g
- Tomato paste — 1.5g
- Sea salt — 0.5g
- Black pepper — 0.5g
- Water — 6.5g

### Allergens
- **Contains:** None

### Dietary Flags
- GF: Yes | DF: Yes | LF: Yes

### Nutrition Per 50g Serve
| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat | Fiber | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 32 | 1.5g | 3.0g | 1.6g | 0.1g | 320mg |

### INTERNAL — Production Recipe (per 2kg batch)
- Halal beef stock: 800g
- Halal chicken stock: 400g
- Onion, finely diced: 200g
- Fresh garlic, minced: 40g
- Olive oil: 80g
- Cornflour: 80g
- Fresh thyme: 20g
- Tomato paste: 60g
- Sea salt: 20g
- Black pepper: 20g
- Water: 280g

- Shelf life: 7 days refrigerated, 3 months frozen.
- Yield: ~40 portions per 2kg batch.
