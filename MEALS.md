## How to Read Each Meal

- **Sauce:** References a sauce from SAUCES.md by code
- **Method:** Primary cooking method. "Either" means customer picks (instructions for both provided)
- **Dietary:** GF = Gluten Free, DF = Dairy Free, LF = Lactose Free. "Yes" = compliant as-is. "Swap" = compliant with noted ingredient swap.
- **Cook time:** Active cooking only. Zero prep by customer.
- **Nutrition:** Per 1-person serve. Includes sauce.

## Day 1 — Monday

### Lunch: Grilled Chicken Breast with Charred Broccoli and Sweet Potato

**Description:** Butterflied chicken breast rubbed with simple salt, pepper and olive oil, pan-seared until golden. Served with charred broccoli florets and roasted sweet potato wedges.

**Sauce:** `simple-spo` (Salt, Pepper & Olive Oil)
**Method:** Pan / Either (also works in air fryer)
**Cook time:** 18 min
**Difficulty:** Beginner

| Ingredient | Quantity | Category |
|---|---|---|
| Chicken breast | 200g | Protein |
| Broccoli florets | 150g | Vegetable |
| Sweet potato, cut into wedges | 120g | Carb |
| Salt, Pepper & Olive Oil pouch | 15g | Sauce |

**Nutrition (1 serve):**
| Calories | Protein | Carbs | Fat | Fiber | Sodium |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 502 | 67g | 35g | 18g | 8g | 510mg |

**Allergens:** None
**Dietary:** GF: Yes | DF: Yes | LF: Yes

**Cooking steps:**
1. Heat pan to medium-high with oil from sauce pouch
2. Season chicken with salt and pepper from pouch
3. Cook chicken 5-6 min per side until golden and cooked through
4. While chicken cooks, spread sweet potato wedges on a tray, drizzle remaining oil, air fry at 200C for 15 min OR pan alongside chicken
5. Add broccoli to pan in last 5 min, char on high heat
6. Rest chicken 2 min, slice, serve over vegetables

# ORDERING MODEL — CLARIFICATION

> **Users are NOT locked into set plans.** Plans are an option, not a requirement. The ordering system supports:
>
> **1. Set Meal Plans** — Pre-built plans (The Lean Machine, Bulk Up, etc.) ordered as a bundle for 3/5/7 days.
>
> **2. Individual Meal Orders** — Browse the full meal library, pick any meals they want. No plan required. Good for casual users, one-off dinners, or trying before committing to a plan.
>
> **3. Build Your Own Plan** — Pick their own selection of meals for the week. Choose protein, carbs, veg, sauce, cooking method. Set their own macros or let the system suggest.
>
> **Portion Sizing:**
> - **1 person** — Single serve, individually packaged
> - **2 people** — Double serve, packaged as one unit (open, empty, cook)
> - **Family (4 people)** — Four serves, packaged as one unit
>
> **Key rule: Customers choose QUANTITY and PACKAGING separately:**
> - **3 individual meals** = 3 different meals, each packaged individually
> - **3 portions of 1 meal** = Same meal x3, packaged together as a bulk cook unit (e.g. 3 portions of coconut chicken curry — one big pack, cook it all, portion it yourself after)
>
> **Example orders:**
> - "I want Tuesday's dinner for my family" → 1 meal, family (4-person) portion, individually packaged
> - "I want 5 different dinners for the week, just for me" → 5 meals, 1-person each, individually packaged
> - "I want 4 portions of the beef curry so I can meal prep" → 1 meal, 4 portions, bulk packaged together
> - "I want The Lean Machine 5-day plan for 2 people" → Set plan, 10 meals, 2-person portions
>
> **Packaging:**
> - Individual meals: Each ingredient in its own labelled bag inside one meal box. Sauce pouch inside. Recipe card with QR code.
> - Multi-portion bulk: All ingredients scaled and packed together in one larger bag per ingredient. Sauce pouch scaled. Single recipe card. Customer cooks all at once, portions themselves after.
> - All packaging labelled with: meal name, ingredients, allergens, nutrition (per serve), halal certification, pack date, use-by date.
